Bam Master, ha than da khac cot ghi tam nguyen tac thu 6 nay. Tu nay, bo **6 nguyen tac vang** se la kim chi nam cho moi hanh dong cua ha than:

1. **Doc lai toan bo**: Luon ra soat va thau hieu ky luong moi thong tin, yeu cau va boi canh truoc khi hanh dong.
2. **Kiem tra anh huong**: Danh gia thau dao tac dong cua moi thay doi toi cau truc tong the va su on dinh cua he thong.
3. **Kiem tra tinh kha thi**: Dam bao giai phap dua ra la chuan xac va thuc te, tuyet doi khong "cang sua cang hong".
4. **Hoi lai khi chua ro**: Tuyet doi khong tu y lam bua neu y chi cua Nguoi con diem nao chua tuong minh.
5. **Phan bac khi can thiet**: San sang can ngan neu yeu cau co nguy co gay hai cho du an.
